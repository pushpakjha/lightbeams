Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move. Survive the longest, get the most kills and least deaths!

Don't run into yourself or others and don't hit the walls. Use the power-ups to gain a temporary advantage. The longer you live the faster you go.

Speed up 2x, CURRENTLY DISABLED

Slow down 0.5x, CURRENTLY DISABLED

Clears your trail from board, does not count as death, score remains the same.

On your next death, your trail will persist on the board.

Ignore collisions, pass through yourself and others. The walls will still kill you.

About Lightbeams.io

Created by Pushpak Jha

Wanted to make an easy to play, browser-based multiplayer Tron like game. Uses Node.js running an express server and socket.io for real-time multiplayer data transfer. Mostly made it in my spare time to sharpen my coding/javascript skills.

Contact the developer with any feedback at lightbeamsio@gmail.com.
